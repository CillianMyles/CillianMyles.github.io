My name is Cillian and I'm a 24 year old guy living in Galway in the West of Ireland.

I studied Computer and Electronic engineering in National University of Ireland, Galway and am currently employed as a software engineer by OnePageCRM who's offices are based out of the same university.

I have always been an Android user, so I created an Android app for my final year project in college. This sparked a curiosity for me in Android development, which led me to pursue a career as an Android developer.

My interests include app development and design, olympic weightlifting, reading and some gaming from time to time (less than I would like).
